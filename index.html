Molten Lava Cake Recipe

Below you will find the ingredients list and the steps required to make a delicious molten lava cake. Thanks for using our recipe and we hope you enjoy!!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz bittersweet chocolate
  • 4 oz butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder
  • ice cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. In a microwave safe bowl, melt chococlate and butter in 30-second increments
  3. In a seperate large bowl, whisk the eggs ands ugar until pale and fluffy. Whisk in the melted chocolate along with the flour, vanilla, and instant espresso.
  4. Divide butter into ramekins and place them on a baking sheet. Bake 12 minutes or until the edges begin to pull away from the ramekins but the center is still jiggly
  5. Remove from oven and serve immediately with ice cream
